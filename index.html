ABOUT US

We love to solve problems

Bayes Consulting came together unofficially when we (Jay and Matt) met in a computer science course at The University of Texas. We quickly began working on projects together and in 2012, published an article that quantified the relationship between McDonald's locations and diabetes rates. Since then, we've spent our undergraduate careers learning machine learning methods and taking on bigger and tougher challenges.

We started Bayes Consulting after the realization that the most interesting problems to solve aren't behind labratory doors and the biggest questions aren't in academic journals. Since we've come together, we've solved problems at every part of the technology stack and in countless discplines. From predicting happiness to selling concert tickets, we've lost count of the questions we've set out to answer.
